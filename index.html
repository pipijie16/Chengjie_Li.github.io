About Me

I am currently a Master's student in Cartography and Geographical Information System at LIESMARS, Wuhan University, with research interests in 3D city models, 3D urban morphology, urban microclimate, urban building energy modeling, and 3D GIS. My current research focuses on combining the Vertical Greenery System with 3D city models to improve urban environmental modeling. I am supervised by Prof. Zhen Dong, Research Associate Fuxun Liang, and Prof. Bisheng Yang.
